A little about me

Background

I grew up in a small town and developed an early passion for technology and art. In high school, I joined the coding club and fell in love with creating interactive web pages. Learning HTML and CSS felt like discovering a new language that let me build worlds from scratch.

Interests

Outside of computing, I enjoy hiking, photography, and sketching. I often combine these hobbies by taking nature photos and turning them into digital art. These activities help me relax and stay inspired for my next project.

Goals

My goal is to become a front-end developer who builds accessible and responsive websites. I plan to continue learning new frameworks and design techniques while contributing to open source. Creating this personal site is the first step in showcasing my journey.
